<h2>Tourist Information</h2>

<h4>From the Airport</h4>

A taxi costs about 40 CAD. You can also take the Bus 747 for 8 CAD, and then take the metro at Lionel-Groulx or Berri-UQAM metro station.

<h4>Public Transport</h4>

Timetables for buses are available <a href="http://www.stm.info/">here</a>. A map of the metro is available <a href=" http://www.stm.info/metro/mapmetro.htm">here</a>.

<h4>Bike Rental</h4>

A convenient way to visit Montreal is to rent a <a href="http://montreal.bixi.com">bixi bike</a>. It costs 7 CAD for one day, or 15 CAD for 3 days.
